Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

Our AI models achieve 95% accuracy in property valuations by analyzing over 500 market factors including comparable sales, economic indicators, neighborhood trends, and property characteristics. The models are continuously updated with new market data to maintain accuracy.

Use our advanced search filters to find properties by location, price range, property type, and investment criteria. You can also use our "Top Properties" feature to discover undervalued properties with high investment potential identified by our AI.

Free users can access basic property search, AI valuations, and market insights. Premium users get advanced analytics, detailed investment reports, portfolio tracking, priority support, and API access with higher rate limits.

Property data is updated daily from multiple MLS systems across Canada. Economic indicators are updated in real-time from Bank of Canada and Statistics Canada feeds. AI models are retrained weekly with new market data to ensure predictions remain current.

Currently, NextProperty AI focuses on residential real estate investment. We analyze single-family homes, condos, townhouses, and small multi-family properties. Commercial real estate features are planned for future releases.

Investment scores range from 1-10, with 10 being the highest potential. Scores consider factors like price vs. market value, rental yield potential, neighborhood growth, and market trends. Properties scoring 7+ are generally considered good investment opportunities.
